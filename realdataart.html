WORLD WIDE WASTE

Hyper Island's Digital Data Strategy students exhibited their digital data based art work on global issues focussing on how to change peoples' attitude and behaviour in the Kleerup Gallery in Stockholm. Some 70 guests attended the DDS students' exhibition in the Kleerup Gallery and enjoyed the critical and inspiring story-telling about change. The work was the result of a 72-hour workshop with Stefanie Posavec, a London-based artist working across data visualisation. Wide range of subjects covering data visualisation for freedom of speech, gay marriage, noise pollution, "thirsty meat", world wide waste, clothing consumption, overconsumption, metabolism in relation to eating breakfast, average distance to clean water and alcohol abuse.
