MEET OUR PEOPLE

During the our ART module together with 'Information is beautiful' at Hyper Island, I created together with 4 other fellow students a digital visualisation of the complete Hyper Island network. We built our visualisation on a PhiloGL base and spent our days discovering a lot of new Javascript and Github tools. To get the complete database of the Hyper Island network, we built a Python syntax and made it run through the scraperwiki website. Combining the data-set with geo-locations and some nice css, gave us this final result!
