INTERNATIONAL RESCUE COMITTEE

Together with the International Rescue Committee and idea shop Gyro, Hyper Island gathered 447 students at their campuses in Stockholm and Karlskrona - to create, discuss and work on ideas for how to inform the world about domestic violence in developing countries. Flip-Flop Interactive was proud to be apart of this 72 hour project.
