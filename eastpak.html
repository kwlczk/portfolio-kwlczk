Between 2005 and 2011 I've been working as a product designer and product developer for Eastpak! In this role, I've been able to help build the brand Eastpak with new strategies, brand directions, but more heavinly put my weight on the trend report and design direction of the brand. During that time, we've seen the brand evolve from streetwear brand to a brand with credible fashion collaborations and up-to-date designs. I've been able to enjoy collaborations with RAF SIMONS, RICK OWENS, QUINZE AND MILAN, KRIS VAN ASSCHE, WOOD WOOD, GASPARD YURKIEVICH, ELLEY KISHIMOTO, ...
